Using 'continue' and 'break' in do-while loop:

As with 'continue' and 'break' in 'while' statement, they do similar change in control flow even in do-while statement. Using 'continue' will execute next iteration of do-while loop, where as 'break' will pass on the control flow following the do-while loop.
